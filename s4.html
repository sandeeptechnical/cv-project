ASYNCHRONOUS E-LEARNING

E-learning where people are not online at same time and interaction does not occur at a time, allowing people to perticipate on their scedules. the learner is free to decide when he or she want to study.

characteristics for ASYNCHRONOUS learning is that fact the trainer prepare the course material before the course takes place. example:videotaped classes, stored audio-video web presentation ,q & a mentoring reading E-mail massages.
